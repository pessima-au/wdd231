Site Plan for eBook Reading and Sharing Website

Site Name

Name: E-Reader Haven

Reason: This name represents a digital haven for book lovers who want a seamless online expperience searching for ebooks

Site Purpose

E-Read Haven will provide users with an informative and engaging platform dedicated to eBooks. The website will offer the following features:

  • Information about various eBooks and their genres
  • Book reviews and recommendations
  • Contact and support resources
  • A community forum for book discussions

Scenarios

  1. Where can I find recommendations for the best eBooks in my favorite genre?
  2. Where can I find interesting books to read free of charge?

Color Schema

#333333
#F5F5F5
#424939

Usage:

  • Primary Color - Dark Gray [#333333]: Background color for the header and footer, navigation bar. Also used as text color in non-white dark backgrounds.
  • Secondary color - Dark olive green [#424939]: Used for primary call-to-action buttons, borders, dividers, active Links/hover Effects.
  • Background Color - Light Gray [#F5F5F5]: Background for content sections like the hero and photo containers.

Typography

  • Heading Font - "Merriweather", serif: Used for headings to give a classic, book-like feel.
  • Body Font - "Lato", sans-serif: Used for body text to ensure readability and modern design and simplicity.
  • Accent Font - Comic Neue: Used sparingly in the community/library pages.
